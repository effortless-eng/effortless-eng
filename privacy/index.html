By accessing or using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

How This Privacy Policy Applies #

This Privacy Policy applies to all Effortless websites, web services, desktop applications, and mobile applications (collectively referred to as “Services”). By using our Services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect and How We Use It #

Personal Data: We may collect and process certain personally identifiable information (“Personal Data”) that can be used to contact or identify you. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Name
  • Company name
  • Email address
  • IP address
  • Device information (e.g., computer name, browser version, operating system)
  • Usage data (e.g., pages visited, time and date of visit, time spent on pages)

Legal Basis for Processing: We process your Personal Data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent: For marketing communications and non-essential cookies, we obtain your explicit consent.
  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill our contractual obligations to you, such as providing our services or processing your orders.
  • Legal Obligation: For processing necessary to comply with legal obligations, such as accounting and auditing.

How We Collect Your Data #

We collect data directly from you when you:

  • Register online or place an order for our products and services
  • Voluntarily complete a customer survey or provide feedback
  • Use our website, web services, desktop, and mobile applications

How We Use Your Data #

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To process orders, manage your account, provide support, and fulfill our contractual obligations.
  • Marketing: With your consent, to send you special offers, updates, and marketing communications.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage data and improve our services and user experience.
  • Security: To ensure the security and integrity of our services.
  • Analytics and Statistics: To generate aggregate statistical information and perform market research.
  • Fraud Prevention: To verify the authenticity of our services and prevent fraud.

Data Retention #

We retain your Personal Data as follows:

  • Accounting and Auditing: Indefinitely, as required by legal obligations.
  • Other Processing: Until you request the deletion of your data or until it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.

Data Sharing and Transfers #

Third-Party Processors: We may share your data with third-party service providers that assist us in delivering our services. These include:

  • Hosting Providers: Such as Microsoft Azure.
  • Payment Processors: Such as Stripe, Paddle.
  • Analytics Services: Such as Google Analytics.
  • Cloud Services: Such as Google Drive API.

Data Transfers Outside the EU: If your data is transferred outside the European Union, we apply our best efforts to ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses or other legally recognized transfer mechanisms, to protect your Personal Data.

Data Security #

We take the security of your Personal Data seriously and implement commercially reasonable measures to protect it. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Data Protection Rights #

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request copies of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request restriction of your data processing.
  • Right to Object: Object to the processing of your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another organization or directly toyou. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through our contact form.

Your continued use of our Services after such changes constitutes your acknowledgment and consent to the updated Privacy Policy. If we make any material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you through the email address you have provided or by placing a prominent notice on our website.

Contact Us #

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your rights under GDPR, please contact us through our contact form.
